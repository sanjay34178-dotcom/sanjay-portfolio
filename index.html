Experience & Notable Projects

Medical VR Anatomy Training Application – Unreal Engine

Sri Lalithambigai Medical College & Hospital (Dr MGR Educational and Research Institute) | Sep 2025 – Feb 2026 | Real-Time Medical VR Project

Collaborated on the development of an interactive VR application designed to help medical students study human anatomy in an immersive 3D environment. Sculpted high-resolution anatomical models including heart, brain, stomach, and liver in ZBrush, focusing on anatomical accuracy, proportion, and refined surface detailing. Converted high-poly sculpts into optimized game-ready assets through retopology and efficient UV mapping. Assisted in texture baking and PBR material setup for real-time performance, and integrated assets into Unreal Engine for VR interaction and real-time rendering.

Predator-Inspired Character & Sci-Fi Spaceship

Personal Project | 2026

Designed and sculpted a high-detail sci-fi character inspired by the Predator universe, along with a complementary spaceship asset to build a cohesive visual narrative. Sculpted a high-resolution creature character in ZBrush, focusing on anatomy, muscular structure, and surface detailing. Developed intricate hard-surface elements including armor and mechanical components. Performed retopology and UV mapping for optimized real-time performance, baked normal maps, and created cinematic lighting and presentation renders for portfolio display.

Level Design Practice – Unreal Engine & Blender

Personal Project | 2025 – 2026

Designed a forest environment level in Unreal Engine as part of a semester project, focusing on environment composition, lighting, and scene layout. Developed street and bar environment concepts using Blender and Autodesk Maya. Practiced level layout, prop placement, and atmosphere creation to support storytelling in real-time environments. Explored lighting and scene composition techniques for immersive environment presentation.
